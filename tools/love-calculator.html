Frequently Asked Questions

Is this love calculator scientifically accurate?
No, this love calculator is purely for entertainment purposes. It uses a fun algorithm based on the letters in both names to generate a compatibility percentage. It's not based on any scientific or psychological principles and should be taken as a lighthearted game rather than a serious assessment of relationship compatibility.
Will I get the same result every time?
Yes, for the same pair of names, you will get the same result every time. Our algorithm uses consistent calculations based on the letters in the names. However, even small changes like using nicknames or middle names can produce different results.
Should I make relationship decisions based on this calculator?
Absolutely not! This calculator is designed purely for fun and entertainment. Real relationships are complex and depend on many factors like communication, shared values, trust, and mutual respect. Never make important relationship decisions based on a fun online calculator.
Can I check compatibility between friends or family members?
Yes! While it's called a "love" calculator, you can use it to check the name compatibility between any two people - friends, family members, colleagues, or even celebrities. It's all in good fun!
