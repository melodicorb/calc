About Speed, Distance & Time

Speed, distance, and time are related by a simple formula: Speed = Distance / Time. This relationship allows you to calculate any one of these variables if you know the other two.

Speed Units

  • Kilometers per hour (km/h) - Common for road speeds in most countries
  • Meters per second (m/s) - Standard unit in physics
  • Miles per hour (mph) - Used in the US, UK, and some other countries
  • Feet per second (ft/s) - Used in some engineering applications

Practical Applications

  • Travel Planning: Calculate arrival times or required speeds for journeys
  • Sports: Measure athletic performance in running, cycling, swimming, etc.
  • Physics: Solve motion problems in academic or professional settings
  • Navigation: Plan routes and estimate travel durations
