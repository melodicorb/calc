Formula Reference

Square

Area: A = s²

Perimeter: P = 4s

Where s is the side length

Rectangle

Area: A = l × w

Perimeter: P = 2(l + w)

Where l is length and w is width

Circle

Area: A = πr²

Circumference: C = 2πr

Where r is radius and π ≈ 3.14159

Triangle

Area: A = √(s(s-a)(s-b)(s-c))

Perimeter: P = a + b + c

Where a, b, c are sides and s = (a+b+c)/2

Tips & Notes

  • All measurements should be in the same unit (e.g., cm, m, in, ft).
  • For triangles, make sure the sides can form a valid triangle (sum of any two sides must be greater than the third side).
  • Results are rounded to 2 decimal places for better readability.
  • For more complex shapes, consider breaking them down into simpler shapes and calculating separately.
