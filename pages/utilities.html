Frequently Asked Questions

How does the Age Calculator work?
The Age Calculator takes your birth date and calculates the exact time that has passed until the current date (or another specified date). It accounts for leap years, different month lengths, and can display your age in various formats including years, months, days, hours, minutes, and even seconds.
Is the Love Calculator scientifically accurate?
No, the Love Calculator is purely for entertainment purposes. It uses a fun algorithm based on the letters in both names to generate a compatibility percentage. It's not based on any scientific or psychological principles and should be taken as a lighthearted game rather than a serious assessment of relationship compatibility.
How secure is the Password Strength Checker?
Our Password Strength Checker evaluates passwords based on length, complexity, character variety, and common patterns. All processing is done locally in your browser - we never store or transmit your passwords to any server. The tool provides feedback on how to improve weak passwords and estimates how long it might take for a password to be cracked using current computing technology.
Can I download the QR codes I generate?
Yes, our QR Code Generator allows you to download the generated QR codes as PNG or SVG files. You can also adjust the size and color of the QR code before downloading. The generated QR codes can be used for websites, business cards, promotional materials, or any other application where you need to share information quickly.
How is the Date Difference Calculator useful?
The Date Difference Calculator is useful for many purposes, such as calculating project timelines, determining contract durations, planning events, calculating age, tracking pregnancy weeks, or finding out how many days until a special occasion. It provides the difference in various units (years, months, weeks, days) and accounts for leap years and different month lengths.
